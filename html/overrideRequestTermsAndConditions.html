Terms and Conditions

Department of Mathematics, Computer Science, and Statistics

While the Departments of Mathematics, Computer Science, and Statistics have implemented wait lists for some of their courses, they are offered strictly on a first come, first served basis (there is no re-ordering or prioritizing of the wait lists). There is no mechanism to request priority consideration in a wait list.

However, if you have EXCEPTIONAL NEED for a course, you may use this form to instead request a registration override, which will allow you immediate registration access.
If your need is not exceptional, you should stop now.
You may still place your name on a wait list if one exists, take a different section if one is open, or delay taking the course to a later semester.

INSTRUCTIONS:

If your request is granted, you will receive an email and your request will be archived in your Override Profile

Granted: Overrides will be granted on the basis of EXCEPTIONAL NEED after all information has been verified by our office for accuracy.

Denied: If your request is denied, you will be notified by an email from the department office administrative assistant.

Most override requests are granted or denied within 2 days.

If the override is approved, the student has 3 DAYS to add the course. After three days, the override may be revoked. If the student applies the override after the Free Add/Drop Period, a $50 fee will be assessed to the studentâ€™s account regardless of when the override was granted.
