Terms and Conditions

  • Wait Lists are offered strictly on a first come, first served basis (there is no re-ordering or prioritizing of the wait lists). There is no mechanism to request priority consideration in a wait list.
  • Override Requests will only be considered on the basis of exceptional need. If you do not have exceptional need, do not submit an override request.
  • If you've submitted an override request, you can still place your name on a wait list if one exists, take a different section if one is open, or delay the course to a later semester.
  • Most override requests are granted or denied within 2 business days.
  • If the request is approved and entered into Banner, the student has 3 calendar days to add the course.
    • After 3 days, the override is subject to being revoked.
    • If the student applies the override after the free add/drop period, a $50 fee will be assessed to the student's account, regardless of when the override was granted.
